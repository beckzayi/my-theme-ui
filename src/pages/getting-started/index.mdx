# Getting Started

Follow our simple list below to get up & running with the enableHR API.

#### 1. Get setup

- Signup for an enablehr account
- Add your OAuth 2.0 application (you'll be prompted to login to enableHR first)

#### 2. Explore

- Review the scopes your app will need to use
- Use the Postman collection to get familiar with authentication

#### 3. Integrate

- Check if we have a how-to guide for what you want to do
- Refer to our documentation
- Get your customers using it.

#### 4. Partner with us

- [Become an enableHR Partner ](https://www.enablehr.com.au/partner)
- Contact us and share your use case
